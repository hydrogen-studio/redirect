North America - Salt Lake City

With the beautiful salt lake, KSLC is truly a beautiful destinations for travelers and crews to enjoy spending time in!

North America - Nashville

Being the home of music in America, travelers and crews can have fun in music festivals and enjoy the work of art my various artists!

North America - Los Angeles

Los Angeles, our international hub connecting international destinations in Asia, Oceania, and Europe, is the best destination for families with children, as KLAX is close to Universal Studios and Disneyland.

North America - New York

Main hub in the US east coast, handling traffics from Europe, South America and North America services, is a popular destinations for summer vacation, being so close to Manhattan.

North America - Anchorage

Being our upcoming cargo hub, Anchorage truly showed enough amazement for pilots to fly into! Enjoy the beautiful views of the infamous snowy mountain!

Europe - London

Being our main Europe hub, London Heathrow receives routes that connects routes all across EU and also connects with North America and Asia.

Oceania - Sydney 

Currently our only hub in Oceania, this hub is aiming to serve routes in Australia and New Zealand!

Asia - Beijing

Beijing serves as a central hub in our Asia Branch, air Folklore, it connects countries like Japan, Korea and China together, with planned routes connecting North America and Europe.

South America -Guarulhos

Serving as the hub for South America, Guarulhos connects cities in the continent of South America, and connecting international routes to North America and Oceania.

Fleets

Fly a variety of aircraft types from Airbus to Boeing

Airbus A320 Ceo

Manages the short and medium haul operations of vEvermore throughout the 5 continents we operate in. Airbus A320 Ceo is truly a masterpiece for our daily operations. 

Airbus A320 Neo

Airbus A320 Neo, Airbus updated A320 that is aimed to reduce CO2 emission while being efficient, serves our short to long haul branch mainly in North America.

Airbus A321 Ceo

Mainly serving our trans-continental routes between the US west coast and US east coast, Airbus A321 Ceo helped us dramatically with our efforts in reducing Climate Change.

Airbus A321 Neo

Similar to A320 Neo, Airbus A321 Neo also aims to reduce CO2 emission while providing airlines with fuel efficiency. A321 Neo in our fleet serves as Atlantic Crossing and in our California to Alaska routes.

Airbus A350

Serving as backbone of our long haul operations connecting our 6 International Hubs! The A350 provides travelers with the best cabin comfort.

Boeing B737 Family

From the -700 to -800, enjoy short and medium haul routes with the classical cockpit.

VATSIM

A nonprofit organization that operates an online flight-simulation network noted for its active membership and realism.

IVAO

The International Virtual Aviation Organisation, IVAO for short, was founded in 1998 to provide an online platform for flight simulation enthusiasts to enjoy their hobby in a simulated real-world environment, in company of other people, flying or providing Air Traffic Control services.
